About Us

Servi-Tek first opened its doors in 2006 with one office and one simple goal: to partner with local business owners throughout Southern California to deliver superior engineering and facilities management solutions that set us apart from other providers.

Servi-Tek has grown and matured over the last 12 years, currently operating in five locations and servicing clients across four states. However, our vision remains the same. Servi-Tek still teams with businesses of every size and scope to develop innovative, customized engineering and facilities management strategies that optimize efficiencies and maximize overall business value.
